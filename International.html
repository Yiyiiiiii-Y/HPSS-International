About HPSS

HPSS is located in the heart of the Hobsonville Point community in Auckland, with a beautiful surrounding environment and convenient transportation. The school adheres to the concepts of innovation, participation and inspiration, and utilizes advanced facilities to provide personalized learning opportunities for students. The school's curriculum includes learning centers, professional learning and project-based learning, aiming to cultivate students' critical thinking, innovation ability and social responsibility.

What is the HUB?

Hubs are central to HPSS’s learner-centred approach. Each student has a Hub Coach for academic and pastoral support, helping them set goals, reflect on learning, and develop social-emotional skills. Communities group several hubs under a Lead Teacher, fostering collaboration, belonging, and leadership. Together, Hubs and Communities provide personalised support for both learning and well-being.
